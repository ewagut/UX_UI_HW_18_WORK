My Work

TripIt

Planning and budgeting for vacation can be overwhelming and time consuming. Many travelers find that they end up spending well over their budget after they travel. Yet, studies have shown that vacation time can be beneficial for physical and mental health. Our goal is to create an app that helps individuals easily find flights, accommodations and activities during their vacation and allows them to track their expenses in one place.

Waste Smart

An issue most of us face- food waste. Food waste in our homes makes up about 39% of all food waste in the US- about 42 billion pounds of food waste. This app will help users track of persihable items in their homes, helping them save money and reduce waste.

Felines and Canines

A redesign of a local non-profit to bolster the sites warmth and trust to the user that encourages donations and adoptions. Responsive web design for desktop and phone.
